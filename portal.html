Platform Features

Everything Your Plant Needs

Built by people who understand pellet manufacturing. Every feature solves a real problem.

⚖

Weighbridge Management

Log vehicle entries, auto-calculate moisture deductions, track inbound raw materials and outbound dispatches with full traceability.

🏭

Production Tracking

Shift-based logging with per-machine breakdowns. Know exactly how much each machine produced, downtime reasons, and capacity utilization.

🔬

Quality Testing

Record GCV, moisture, ash content, and bulk density for every batch. Auto-flag non-compliant batches against NTPC specs.

🚚

Dispatch & Invoicing

Generate dispatch orders, track delivery status, and create GST-compliant invoices — all from the same screen.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

KPI dashboards showing capacity utilization, quality benchmarks, revenue trends, and 30-day production vs dispatch analysis.

📱

Mobile Worker App

Field-optimized interface with OTP login, large touch targets, and offline support. Workers see only what they need.
