About Kanoz Bio Energy

Building India's Clean Energy Future

Our Process

From Field to Fuel

A streamlined manufacturing process that ensures quality at every step, from raw material collection to final delivery.

1

Raw Material Collection

Agricultural residues sourced directly from local farmers — crop stalks, husks, and other biomass waste.

2

Processing & Drying

Raw materials are cleaned, shredded, and dried to optimal moisture levels for pellet formation.

3

Pelletization

High-pressure pellet mills compress the biomass into uniform, dense pellets with consistent calorific value.

4

Quality & Dispatch

Every batch is tested for moisture, ash content, and calorific value before dispatch to customers.

Sustainability

Good for Business, Better for the Planet

Every ton of biomass pellets used instead of coal reduces carbon emissions significantly. By converting agricultural waste into fuel, we also help reduce crop stubble burning — one of India's major pollution contributors.

Our pellets offer a cost-effective, carbon-neutral energy source that helps industries meet sustainability goals while reducing fuel costs.
